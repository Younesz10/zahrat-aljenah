About Zahrat Al Jenah Co.

Your Trusted Partner in Household Cleaning Excellence

Our Story

Zahrat Al Jenah Co. is a pioneering force in the household cleaning product industry. With a steadfast commitment to quality and innovation, we have rapidly grown to become a trusted name in Kurdistan, Iraq, and beyond.

Founded with a vision to bring superior cleaning solutions to every home, we have built our reputation on the pillars of quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction. Our journey from a local startup to a regional leader reflects our dedication to excellence and our deep understanding of our customers' needs.

Our Mission

At Zahrat Al Jenah Co., we are dedicated to simplifying and enhancing the lives of our customers through superior cleaning solutions. By consistently delivering products of the highest quality, we aim to foster cleanliness, comfort, and well-being in every home we touch.

Our Presence

With our main office located in Zakho and a secondary office in Duhok, both nestled in the heart of Kurdistan, Iraq, Zahrat Al Jenah Co. proudly serves the local community and beyond.
